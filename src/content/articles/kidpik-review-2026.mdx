## What is Kidpik?

Kidpik is a personalized styling service for children (sizes 2T to 16 and shoes sizes 7 to 6). Unlike a traditional online store, Kidpik uses a "style quiz" to understand your child's unique personality and preferences, then delivers a curated "pik" of head-to-toe outfits directly to your door.

Founded in 2016 by Ezra Dabah (formerly of The Children's Place), Kidpik has shipped over 1.3 million boxes, establishing itself as a leader in the kids' subscription box space. Their mission is simple: to help parents save time while empowering kids to discover their own unique personal style.

## How Does Kidpik Work?

The Kidpik experience is designed to be as "effortless, fun, and surprising" as possible. Here is the step-by-step breakdown:

1.  **Take the 3-Minute Style Quiz:** You and your child share preferences regarding colors, prints, fits, and styles (e.g., Classic, Trendy, Active, or Glam).
2.  **Get a Personalized Box:** Kidpik’s expert stylists select 8 items (including shoes and accessories) based on the quiz results.
3.  **Try On at Home:** You have 7 days to try everything on in the comfort of your home.
4.  **Keep What You Love, Return the Rest:** Only pay for the items you decide to keep. If you keep the entire box, you get a significant discount.

## What’s Inside a Kidpik Box?

Each Kidpik box typically contains about 8 items of high-quality, in-house designed apparel, shoes, and accessories. Because Kidpik designs their own collections, they can maintain a level of "mix-and-match" versatility that is hard to find with mixed-brand boxes.

Typical items include:
*   Graphic tees and fashionable tops
*   Denim, leggings, and skirts
*   Dresses and rompers
*   Jackets and outerwear
*   Shoes (sneakers, boots, sandals)
*   Accessories (hats, jewelry, hair clips)

## Kidpik Pricing: Is It Affordable?

One of the best features of Kidpik is its transparent pricing model. 

*   **No Styling Fee:** Unlike many other subscription services (like Stitch Fix), Kidpik does not charge a styling fee.
*   **Average Item Cost:** The average item costs about **$24**.
*   **The "Keep-All" Discount:** If you decide to keep the entire box, you receive a **30% discount**, bringing the average item cost down to approximately **$18**.
*   **Shipping & Returns:** Both are always free. A prepaid return envelope is included in every box.
