## 4. Tips for Smart Shopping in 2026

*   **The "Never Buy Used" Exception:** We generally advise against buying used car seats from unverified individuals (like on Facebook Marketplace) because you cannot guarantee the accident history. **Only** buy used car seats from certified programs like **Stork Exchange** or **Rebelstork**.
*   **Wait for Sales Cycles:** If you must buy new, wait for the major 2026 sales cycles: Black Friday (November), Amazon Prime Day (July), and the "Spring Refresh" clearance sales in late March.
*   **Bundle and Save:** Many brands offer significant discounts (often \$100+) when you buy a "travel system" (stroller + car seat) rather than individual pieces.
