For modern parents in 2026, the convenience of a diaper subscription is a must—but so is environmental responsibility. With billions of diapers ending up in landfills every year, choosing an eco-friendly option is one of the biggest impacts a nursery can make.

However, "eco-friendly" can mean anything from bamboo-based fabrics to compostability. To help you choose, we’ve reviewed the three leaders in the space: **Dyper**, **The Honest Company**, and **Coterie**. Here is how they stack up in 2026.

## 1. Dyper: The Leader in Compostability

If your primary goal is to keep diapers out of landfills entirely, **Dyper** is the gold standard. Their bamboo-based diapers are designed to be composted through their proprietary "Redyper" service.

### Key Features:
- **Bamboo Construction:** Naturally hypoallergenic and highly absorbent.
- **REDYPER Service:** For an additional fee, Dyper will pick up used diapers and transport them to a commercial composting facility.
- **Subscription Perks:** Easy to pause, skip, or accelerate deliveries via their app.

**Best for:** Parents committed to a zero-waste lifestyle who are willing to pay a premium for composting services.

## 2. The Honest Company: Style and Sustainability

Founded by Jessica Alba, **The Honest Company** has been a pioneer in the clean baby space for over a decade. In 2026, their diapers are more absorbent than ever while maintaining their signature cute prints and plant-based construction.

### Key Features:
- **Clean Conscious Diapers:** Made with a bio-based core and sustainably harvested fluff pulp.
- **Design Options:** Hundreds of rotating seasonal prints that make diaper changes a bit more fun.
- **Bundle & Save:** Their subscription model allows you to mix and match diaper sizes and wipes for significant savings.

**Best for:** Parents who want a reliable, clean diaper with the convenience of a widely available brand and the fun of stylish prints.

## 3. Coterie: The High-Performance Premium Choice

**Coterie** positions itself as the "luxury" diaper, but their eco-credentials are just as impressive as their performance. They focus on using the cleanest possible materials to prevent diaper rash and maximize sleep time.

### Key Features:
- **Superior Absorbency:** Designed to keep babies dry for up to 12 hours, making them excellent for overnight use.
- **Clean Materials:** TCF (Totally Chlorine Free), non-toxic, and incredibly soft—often described as feeling like "cashmere for your baby."
- **Eco-Focus:** They use 25% plant-based materials and are 100% recyclable in certain markets.

**Best for:** Parents who prioritize performance and skin health, and want the softest possible materials for their baby.
