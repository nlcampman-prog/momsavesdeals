## FAQ: Finding the Best Stroller Deals
### When is the best time to buy a stroller?
Historically, **Black Friday** and **Amazon Prime Day** offer the deepest discounts. However, many brands launch new models in the spring, leading to clearance sales on the previous year's models in late winter.
### Are "Amazon Warehouse" strollers safe?
Yes, usually. These are often just returns with damaged packaging. Amazon inspects them to ensure all parts are present and functional. It's a great way to save 30% or more.
### Do I need a travel system?
A travel system (stroller + car seat) can save you money upfront, but ensure both components are highly rated. Sometimes it's better to buy a great stroller and a compatible adapter for your preferred car seat.
