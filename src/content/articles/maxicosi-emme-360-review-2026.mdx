Most car seats serve a single stage: infant, convertible, or booster. The Emme 360 is designed to be the only car seat you ever buy. It covers three distinct stages:

1.  **Rear-Facing:** 5–40 lbs (2.3–18 kg)
2.  **Forward-Facing:** 22–65 lbs (10–29 kg)
3.  **Belt-Positioning Booster:** 40–100 lbs (18–45 kg)

But the real star of the show is the **360° rotation**. With a simple handle at the top of the seat, you can swivel the seat toward the car door. This makes loading and unloading your child exponentially easier, especially in tight parking spots or for parents with chronic back pain.

## Key Features and Benefits

### 1. 360° Swivel (The Game Changer)
The rotation feature is smooth and can be operated with one hand. Unlike some competitors that only rotate in rear-facing mode, the Emme 360 allows you to rotate the seat toward you in both rear and forward-facing configurations. Note: the seat must be locked into either the rear or forward-facing position before driving.

### 2. TensionFix System
One of the most common causes of car seat failure is improper installation (loose belts). Maxi-Cosi has addressed this with the **TensionFix system**. This built-in tensioning tool features a red-to-green indicator that clearly shows you when your vehicle belt is tight enough. It takes the guesswork out of the installation process.

### 3. All-in-One Longevity
Because it converts into a belt-positioning booster, the Emme 360 can stay in your car for up to a decade. The high-quality fabrics and durable chassis are built to withstand the wear and tear of a growing child.

### 4. PureCosi Fabrics
Maxi-Cosi uses **PureCosi** fabrics, which are made without wool or added fire-retardant chemicals. They are incredibly soft to the touch and, more importantly, the cover is **machine washable and dryer safe**. The "QuickClean" design allows you to zip the cover off without having to uninstall the entire seat—a lifesaver after a mid-trip blowout.

## Installation Tips

To get the most out of your Emme 360, follow these 2026 best practices:
- **Use the Belt:** While it has LATCH connectors, the TensionFix system is specifically designed for use with the vehicle’s seat belt. This often provides a more secure fit in most modern cars.
- **Check the Indicator:** Always ensure the TensionFix window is fully green before placing your child in the seat.
- **Recline Matters:** Use the 5-position recline to ensure your infant's head doesn't slump forward, which is critical for maintaining an open airway.

## FAQ: Your Maxi-Cosi Emme 360 Questions Answered

### Can the Emme 360 rotate while the car is moving?
No. The seat must be locked into either the rear-facing or forward-facing position before the vehicle is in motion. The rotation is strictly for loading and unloading.

### Does it fit in a small SUV or sedan?
The Emme 360 is a large seat. While it fits in most vehicles, it may require the front passenger seat to be moved forward significantly in smaller sedans.

### Is the fabric breathable?
Yes, the PureCosi fabrics are designed for moisture-wicking and airflow, keeping your child cool during long summer drives.
