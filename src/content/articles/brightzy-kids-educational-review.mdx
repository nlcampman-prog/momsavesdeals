## Why Tactile Learning Wins in 2026
While there are thousands of educational apps, child development experts in 2026 continue to emphasize the importance of "gross motor learning." Holding a card, pointing to a poster, and physical sorting activities engage more areas of the brain than tapping a screen. Brightzy tools encourage the social-emotional connection between parent and child that is the true foundation of learning.
