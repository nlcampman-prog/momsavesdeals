## FAQ: Baby & Maternity Essentials

### Is hormone tracking at home accurate?
Yes, systems like **Mira Fertility** use the same fluorescence-based technology found in many professional laboratories, providing quantitative data that is far more reliable than traditional LH strips.

### Are weighted sleep sacks safe?
When used correctly according to the manufacturer's weight and age guidelines, weighted sleep sacks are a safe way to help soothe babies. However, you should always consult your pediatrician and ensure your baby can roll over independently if using a sleep sack.

### Can I trust used car seats from resale sites?
We only recommend buying used car seats from certified platforms like **Stork Exchange**, which have a strict multi-point inspection process and a guaranteed history of being accident-free. Never buy a used car seat from an unverified individual.
