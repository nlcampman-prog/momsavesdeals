## Family Carpet Cleaning Guide: Pro Tips
### Deep Clean vs. Spot Clean
- **Deep Cleaning:** Should be done 2-4 times a year (more if you have allergies). This involves moving furniture and cleaning the entire floor surface.
- **Spot Cleaning:** Should be done immediately. The longer a stain sits, the harder it is to remove.

### Maintenance Hacks
To keep your machine running for years:
1.  **Rinse the Dirty Tank:** Always rinse the dirty water tank after every use to prevent odors.
2.  **Clean the Brushes:** Most machines allow you to pop off the brush cover. Remove hair and debris so the brushes can rotate freely.
3.  **Use the Right Solution:** Using generic soap can leave a sticky residue that actually attracts *more* dirt. Stick to the brand-recommended formulas.

## FAQ: Best Carpet Cleaners for Families
### Are carpet cleaners hard to use?
Not anymore. Most modern machines are "one-pass," meaning they wash as you push forward and dry as you pull back.

### Do I really need a separate machine from my vacuum?
Yes. Vacuums only remove dry debris. Carpet cleaners use water and solution to lift deep-seated allergens, bacteria, and liquid stains that a vacuum can't touch.

### Can I clean my sofa with these?
Most of the upright models on this list come with a hose attachment designed specifically for upholstery.
