## 2. Earth Mama Organics "New Mama" Bundle
Postpartum recovery is often overlooked at baby showers. Earth Mama’s bundles include their famous nipple butter, perineal spray, and sitz bath herbs—all organic and toxin-free.
- **Why it’s a great gift:** It shows you care about the mama’s recovery just as much as the baby’s arrival.

## 3. Hatch Rest+ 2nd Gen Sleep Machine
A sound machine, night light, and "time-to-rise" indicator all in one. The Hatch Rest+ is a staple in modern nurseries for a reason.
- **Why it’s a great gift:** It helps set a consistent sleep routine from day one.

## 4. Kyte Baby Bamboo Sleep Bag
Bamboo fabric is incredibly soft, breathable, and temperature-regulating. Kyte Baby’s sleep bags are a favorite for parents who want to ensure their baby stays at the perfect temperature all night.
- **Why it’s a great gift:** It’s a touch of luxury that is also incredibly practical for safe sleep.

## 5. Ubbi Steel Diaper Pail
Unlike plastic pails that absorb odors over time, the Ubbi is made of steel. It’s sleek, comes in multiple colors, and doesn't require special trash bags.
- **Why it’s a great gift:** It’s the one registry item parents don't realize they need until they *really* need it.

## 6. Copper Pearl Multi-Use Covers
These stretchy fabric covers can be used as a car seat cover, nursing cover, high chair cover, and even a scarf for mama.
- **Why it’s a great gift:** It’s the ultimate "space-saver" for a diaper bag.

## 7. Mushie Silicone Bibs
Easy to clean, dishwasher safe, and aesthetically pleasing. Mushie bibs feature a deep pocket to catch food, making mealtime significantly less messy.
- **Why it’s a great gift:** It’s an affordable add-on gift that parents will use daily once solids start.

## 8. Newton Baby Breathable Crib Mattress
If you want to go in on a "group gift," the Newton mattress is a top choice. It’s 100% breathable and washable from cover to core.
- **Why it’s a great gift:** It provides ultimate peace of mind for new parents worried about SIDS or tummy sleeping.

## 9. Haakaa Manual Breast Pump
A simple, silicone pump that uses natural suction to catch let-down while nursing on the other side.
- **Why it’s a great gift:** It’s a low-tech lifesaver for breastfeeding mamas.

## 10. Amazon Gift Card (with a twist)
Sometimes, the best gift is the freedom to buy what’s left on the registry. Pair a gift card with a small, practical item like a pack of **WaterWipes** for a thoughtful touch.
