## What is Little Spoon?
Little Spoon is a fresh-pressed baby food and toddler meal delivery service. Their mission is simple: to make it easier for parents to feed their children high-quality, nutritious, and diverse foods without the stress of meal prep.

Unlike traditional baby food that is heat-pasteurized (killing many nutrients and flavors), Little Spoon uses High-Pressure Processing (HPP). This cold-press technology keeps the food fresh, vibrant, and packed with vitamins for up to 14 days in the fridge.

### The Product Lineup
Little Spoon has expanded significantly over the years. In 2026, their lineup includes:
- **Babyblends:** Fresh, organic purees for every stage of starting solids.

- **Plates:** Nutritious, hidden-veg meals for toddlers and older kids.
