## FAQ: Mama Mia Covers

Yes! They are machine washable. We recommend washing on a cold, gentle cycle and laying flat to dry to maintain the elasticity of the fabric.

### Is it safe to leave on the car seat while driving?
No. For safety reasons, you should always remove any car seat cover while the vehicle is in motion to ensure proper ventilation and to allow the driver to see the child.
