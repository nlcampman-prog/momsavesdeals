## The "Worry List": Ingredients to Avoid

When reading labels, stay away from these common culprits:
- **Artificial Fragrances:** The #1 cause of skin irritation and allergic reactions.
- **Parabens:** Used as preservatives but linked to endocrine disruption.
- **Phthalates:** Often hidden in "fragrance" and used to make plastics flexible.
- **Sulfates (SLS/SLES):** Harsh foaming agents that can strip the skin of its natural oils.
- **Mineral Oil & Petroleum:** Byproducts of the oil industry that can clog pores.
