## The Science of Deep Pressure: Why It Works

Weighted sleep sacks work on the same principle as weighted blankets for adults. This gentle, distributed weight provides proprioceptive input, which can lower cortisol levels and increase serotonin and melatonin production. 

For a baby, this translates to:
- **A calmer nervous system:** Helping them settle down after a stimulating day.
- **Reduced startle reflex:** Also known as the Moro reflex, which often wakes babies up prematurely.
- **Improved sleep transitions:** Helping them bridge the gap between light and deep sleep cycles without fully waking up.

## Safety First: What Every Parent Needs to Know

Before diving into the reviews, safety is our priority. In 2026, the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) and other safety organizations have provided clearer guidelines on weighted products:

1.  **Correct Sizing is Mandatory:** Never buy a larger size for your baby to "grow into." The weight must be proportionate to the baby's current body weight (typically no more than 10%).
2.  **Independence:** Most experts recommend that babies should be able to roll from back to tummy and back again independently before moving into certain weighted styles.
3.  **Breathability:** Ensure the materials are Oeko-Tex certified or similar, to prevent overheating.

### 2. Dreamland Baby: The Full-Body Gentle Hug

Dreamland Baby is famous for its full-body weighted coverage. Their design features weighted beads evenly distributed across the entire front of the sack, providing a "whole body hug" sensation.

*   **Pros:** Very effective for "velcro babies" who only want to be held.
*   **Cons:** Can be heavier than other options; it is critical to follow their exact weight-based sizing chart.

### 3. Nested Bean: The Targeted Hand-Soothe

Nested Bean takes a lighter approach. Their "Zen Sack" features a small, lightly weighted area on the chest (and sometimes the sides) intended to mimic the feeling of a parent’s hand resting on the baby.

*   **Pros:** Excellent for babies who just need a little "nudge" to stay asleep; very breathable for summer months.
*   **Cons:** Not as "weighted" as the others; may not be enough for babies who need significant deep pressure.
