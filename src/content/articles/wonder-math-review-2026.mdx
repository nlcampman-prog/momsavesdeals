## What is Wonder Math?
Wonder Math is an online, story-based math tutoring program designed specifically for 2nd to 5th graders. Unlike static apps where kids play alone, Wonder Math combines high-end game design with **live, small-group instruction** led by expert teachers.

Each week, students log in to join their "mission team" (a small group of peers) and a "Mission Leader" (their teacher) to solve math-based puzzles that advance a larger narrative story.

## How It Works: The Adventure Begins
The core of Wonder Math’s success in 2026 is its "Narrative-First" approach.

### 1. Immersive Storytelling
Children aren't just doing "fractions." They are space explorers or secret agents using fractions to unlock a vault or power a ship. This context provides an immediate answer to the age-old question: *"When will I ever use this in real life?"*

### 2. Gamified Rewards
As students solve problems, they earn points and badges that allow them to customize their avatars and unlock new parts of the world. This dopamine loop keeps engagement high, even when the math gets challenging.

### 3. Live Teacher Support
The "Mission Leaders" are what set Wonder Math apart from apps like Khan Academy. These teachers are trained to spot where a child is struggling in real-time and provide the specific scaffolding needed to help them move forward without frustration.
