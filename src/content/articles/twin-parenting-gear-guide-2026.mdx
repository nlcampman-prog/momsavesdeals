## FAQ: Twin Parenting Common Questions

**Do twins really have a special bond?**
Yes! While every set of twins is different, many develop a unique way of communicating and comforting each other from a very young age.

**Should I dress them in matching outfits?**
That's entirely up to you! Matching is adorable for photos, but as they get older, many parents find that unique outfits help friends and family (and even themselves) distinguish between the two more easily.

**Can I handle twins alone?**
Absolutely. It takes practice and the right gear (like the pillows and strollers mentioned above), but you will quickly find your rhythm.
